# Value & Wealth Layers

- Wealth & Value happen and exist in layers, for example: Financial, Health, Human, Knowledge, Spiritual... 
- Sometimes value occurs and is stored in only one layer.
- Sometimes value and wealth cannot be transferred to a different layer.
- Other times when value is transferred there is loss in the conversion (like in energy conservation principles, energy can be lost when converting it)
- For example: No matter how much financial wealth, it cannot always be converted to health.
- Human wealth cannot be always converted effectively & efficiently to financial wealth.
- Follow the ratio of energy or value input to output. 
> For example, where there is little energy required to generate value on the other side.
- There are cultures where the financial layer doesn't work, so people get used to considering value on the human layer. The quality of the human circle around one self.
> and a lot of the value transfers happen totally outside of the financial layer.

## Types of Value & Wealth
Human, Financial, Material, Spiritual, Health, Knowledge (intellectual)
